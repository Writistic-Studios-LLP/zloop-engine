## Reporting Violations

### How to Report

**If you experience or witness a violation**:

**Step 1: Document**
- Note what happened
- When it occurred
- Who was involved
- Screenshots/evidence if possible
- How it affected you

**Step 2: Report**

**For most violations**:
```
Email: admin@writiverse.com
Subject: [CODE OF CONDUCT] Violation Report

Include:
- Your name and contact info (can be anonymous)
- Detailed description of incident
- Names/usernames involved
- Links to relevant content
- Evidence/screenshots
- What resolution you seek
```

**For severe issues** (threats, harassment, safety):
```
Email: admin@writiverse.com
Subject: [URGENT] Code of Conduct Violation

Include: Same as above, marked URGENT
Response Time: 24 hours
```

**For Writistic staff violations**:
```
Email: admin@writiverse.com
Subject: [CODE OF CONDUCT] Staff Misconduct Report

Include: Same details
Will be reviewed by: Independent committee
```

### Confidentiality

- Your report will be taken seriously
- Identity kept confidential (unless you consent to sharing)
- Shared only with people investigating
- Investigations are prompt and fair
- You'll receive updates on outcome

### No Retaliation

**You are protected from retaliation** for reporting in good faith:
- No removal for reporting
- No exclusion from community
- No negative consequences for honest reports
- Retaliation is itself a violation

## Enforcement & Consequences

### Consequences for Violations

**Level 1: Verbal Warning**
- Inappropriate but one-time behavior
- Misunderstanding or poor communication
- **Action**: Private message explaining violation
- **Expectation**: Acknowledgment and correction
- **Record**: Documented but not public

**Level 2: Written Warning**
- Repeated violations despite warning
- Disruptive but not abusive behavior
- **Action**: Written notice with specific expectations
- **Expectation**: Behavior change within 2 weeks
- **Record**: Documented, may be mentioned to user

**Level 3: Temporary Suspension**
- Continuing violations after written warning
- Abusive or harassing behavior
- **Action**: Suspended from platform/community (1-4 weeks)
- **Timeline**: Suspension length depends on severity
- **Return**: Conditional on acknowledgment and agreement

**Level 4: Permanent Removal**
- Severe, ongoing, or unrepentant violations
- Threats, harassment, illegal activity
- **Action**: Permanent ban from community
- **Duration**: Indefinite
- **Appeal**: Review after 6 months if requested

### Examples of Escalation

```
Example 1: Unintentional Misunderstanding
- Violation: Slightly dismissive tone in feedback
- Level: 1 (Verbal Warning)
- Outcome: Private message, acknowledged, resolved

Example 2: Repeated Rudeness
- Violation: Consistently dismissive to multiple members
- Level: 2 (Written Warning) 
- Outcome: Formal notice, behavior improves

Example 3: Persistent Harassment
- Violation: Continues targeting same member after warning
- Level: 3 (Suspension)
- Outcome: 1-month suspension, can return with behavioral agreement

Example 4: Serious Harassment or Threats
- Violation: Threatening messages, doxxing, sexual harassment
- Level: 4 (Permanent Removal)
- Outcome: Immediate ban, legal action may follow
```

### Appeals Process

**If you receive a consequence**:

1. **Review Communication** (within 48 hours)
   - You'll receive specific explanation
   - Details of violation
   - Duration if applicable
   - Appeal process information

2. **Request Review** (within 14 days)
   - Email: admin@writiverse.com
   - Subject: [APPEAL] My Code of Conduct Case
   - Provide: Your perspective and any new information
   - Keep: Tone professional and non-confrontational

3. **Independent Review** (within 2 weeks)
   - Independent person reviews your appeal
   - Original enforcement reviewed
   - You may provide additional context
   - Decision communicated in writing

4. **Possible Outcomes**:
   - ‚úÖ Consequence upheld
   - ‚úÖ Consequence modified (reduced)
   - ‚úÖ Consequence overturned
   - ‚úÖ Conditions for early reinstatement
